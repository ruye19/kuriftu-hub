🏨 Hospitality 101

Lesson 1 of 4: Understanding Guest Needs

👋 Welcome to Lesson 1

In this lesson, you’ll learn about identifying and fulfilling the diverse needs of resort guests.

Guests may come for relaxation, adventure, business, or family bonding. It’s crucial to anticipate their needs based on their purpose of visit, cultural expectations, and communication style.

💡 Tip: Always greet guests with a smile and personalized attention — it makes a lasting impression!

Here’s a short checklist for guest interaction:

  • Greet with eye contact and a smile
  • Use guest’s name whenever possible
  • Offer help proactively
  • Listen actively without interrupting
